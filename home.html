Why Football is Important?

Good for communities

Football clubs are important for communities and bring a town/city together. Cheering on your local team, having a cup of tea and a chat provide great socialisaiton and entertainment for all age groups.

Great for kids

Football teaches kids how to work in a team to achieve a common goal. Football is also an areobic exercise, and promotes physical and mental fitness which is great for all ages, especially kids.
